About Us

Located in Calangute, within one kilometer of Calangute Beach and 2.1 km of Candolim Beach, Royal Galaxy Hotel has accommodations with a terrace and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive. The property is around 2.6 km from Baga Beach, 9.3 km from Chapora Fort and 17.7 km from Thivim Railway Station. Basilica of Bom Jesus is 22.5 km away and Church of Saint Cajetan is 22.5 km from the guesthouse.
