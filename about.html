About Royal Galaxy Hotel and Resorts

Every great city deserves a truly great hotel - a special place that reflects the importance and the beauty of its location with world-class luxury and the very finest service.

Royal Galaxy a Five-Star Hotel, calangute is delighted to welcome you to the enchanting capital of goa - a precious jewel in the Indian Ocean, with a glittering cultural heritage and a long, proud history.

The hotel benefits from a prime location by the sea that is in the heart of the business district and buzzing social hotspot. A personal tropical sanctuary that is for escaping the city, it offers some of the finest, modern accommodation in the country, with 200 luxurious guest rooms and suites.

An exciting new dining and social scene has come to life around the hotel's outstanding group of restaurants and bars, and the exclusive Horizon Club Lounge is the largest of its kind in Calangute.

To meet the needs of Calangute's dynamic business community, Royal galaxy Hotel, is pleased to offer the largest and extensive hotel conference and event facilities in the region, which can cater to up to 2,000 guests. Calangute is the gateway city to discovering the rich culture, wildlife, mountains and beaches that make goa so unique.

Royal galaxy Hotel, Calangute, looks forward to welcoming you soon.
