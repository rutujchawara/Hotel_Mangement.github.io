Ocean View Package

Your exclusive escape with magnificent ocean views.

Soak up captivating views of the Indian Ocean from your Ocean View Package Room, adorned with contemporary chic decor. The rooms are thoughtfully designed with tasteful furnishings, relaxing colour palettes, and fitted with state-of-the-art technology, delivering the right blend of leisure resort living and sophistication.

Features
  • Each room offers 48 sqm / 516 sqf of luxury.
  • Stunning views of the Indian Ocean.
  • Plush linens and extra-large towels. Glass-enclosed shower, deep soaking tub and designer bath amenities.
  • Broadband Internet and Wi-Fi access.
